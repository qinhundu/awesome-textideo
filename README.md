## 🎬 Introduction

**Textideo** is an advanced AI video generator platform built for content creators, bloggers, and influencers. Powered by cutting-edge Veo 3.1 Video Model technology, Textideo transforms text prompts into stunning 1080p videos with cinematic quality.

### Why Textideo?

- 🎯 **Built for Creators**: Specifically designed for YouTubers, TikTokers, and Instagram influencers
- 🎨 **Professional Quality**: Hollywood-grade visual storytelling with dynamic camera movements
- ⚡ **Fast Generation**: Create professional videos in minutes, not hours
- 🌍 **Global Reach**: Support for 80+ countries and multiple languages
- 💼 **Commercial Use**: Safe for commercial projects and brand content

### 1. Text to Video 📝→🎬
**Path**: [`/text-to-video`](https://textideo.com/text-to-video)

Transform text prompts into professional videos.

**Best for:**
- YouTube video intros
- TikTok/Reels content
- Product demonstrations
- Educational explainers

**Example Prompt:**
```yaml
Subject: Young entrepreneur working on laptop
Action: Typing, looking at screen, smiling
Style: Modern vlog aesthetic
Mood: Inspiring, productive
Duration: 15 seconds

Camera: Close-up with slight pan
```

### Basic Structure

```yaml
Subject: [Who/What is in the video]
Action: [What happens/movement]
Style: [Visual style/aesthetic]
Mood: [Atmosphere/emotion]
Duration: [Video length]
Lighting: [Lighting setup]
Camera: [Angle/movement]
```

### Template 1: Product Advertisement

```yaml
Subject: Sleek smartphone on rotating pedestal
Action: Smooth 360° rotation with light reflections
Style: Modern commercial photography
Mood: Premium, professional
Duration: 15 seconds
Lighting: Studio lighting with soft shadows
Camera: Close-up, slow pan around product
Background: Clean gradient backdrop
```

## ❓ FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: What video quality does Textideo support?**
A: Textideo generates high-quality 1080p videos with professional cinematography.

**Q: How long can videos be?**
A: Video length varies by use case, typically from 5 seconds to 60 seconds for optimal quality.

**Q: Can I use Textideo videos commercially?**
A: Yes! Textideo videos are safe for commercial use, including advertising and brand content.

**Q: What languages are supported?**
A: Textideo supports 80+ countries and multiple languages for global content creation.

### Technical Questions

**Q: How long does video generation take?**
A: Generation time varies by complexity, typically 1-5 minutes for standard videos.

**Q: What formats can I export?**
A: Export in MP4, MOV, and other common video formats optimized for social platforms.

**Q: Can I customize generated videos?**
A: Yes! Adjust animation styles, durations, effects, and export settings after generation.

**Q: How do I maintain character consistency?**
